The Hedgerow Beef Box

HerefordGrass-fed28 days on the bone 10 kgOne animalSlow grownFreezer ready

One box, one animal, nothing swapped in. Our Herefords graze the limestone pasture above the farm, hang for a full four weeks on the bone, and are cut by Tom on a Thursday. Every joint is wrapped in paper, labelled by hand, and packed so it goes straight into the freezer.

  • Born, reared and finished on this farm — we can tell you the field
  • A proper mix of cuts: roasting joints and steaks, not just mince padding
  • Works out around £9.50 a kilo — less than counter price for the same cuts

Collection

Boxes are cut on Thursdays and ready from Friday morning. We'll keep yours in the chiller for up to a week — just tell us when you're coming. If you're making a trip of it, the café corner does a very good bacon bap and the milk machine takes coins and cards.

Delivery

The van covers Bakewell, Baslow, Tideswell and the villages between on Tuesdays and Fridays, and beef boxes ride free with any veg box round. Further afield we send boxes overnight in wool-insulated packaging, chilled with ice packs — £8.50, or free over £120.
