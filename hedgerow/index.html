Opening hours

When to find us up the track

We close on Mondays because that's when the farm needs us most. The rest of the week the shop, butchery and café corner run to farm time — and the milk vending machine in the yard never shuts before dusk.

  • Shop & butchery — Tuesday to Saturday 9–5, Sunday 10–4
  • Café corner — from 9:30, the kitchen closes at 3
  • Milk vending & egg shed — every day, 7am to 7pm

Our story

Four generations up the same track

Frank and Edith Brindley took the tenancy on this valley farm in 1946 with eleven cows and a borrowed horse. Their great-grandchildren milk here now. The shop came in 1998, when supermarket milk prices left us a choice: sell the herd, or sell what the herd makes ourselves. We chose the second, and we'd choose it again.

"Feed the soil and the soil feeds you. Everything else is arithmetic."

— Frank Brindley, first of the four generations
