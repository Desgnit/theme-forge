Contact the club

We're all volunteers with day jobs, so email beats phone — but on a Sunday morning, just come and find a green jacket at Brook Field.

Safeguarding concerns come first

Worried about a child's welfare — at this club or anywhere? Contact our welfare team at welfare@millbrookjuniors.co.uk. Messages to this address are read only by our two welfare officers, answered within 24 hours, and treated in confidence. In an emergency, always call 999 first.

Matchday mornings

  • Sundays: matches from 9:30am, all pitches. Tea hut opens 9am — card accepted, bacon rolls until they run out.
