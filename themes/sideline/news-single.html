Registration for 2026/27 is open — and three new teams launch

6 July 2026 · by the committee · 3 min read

Registration for the 2026/27 season is open from today, and this year we grow again: three new teams join the club, taking us to twenty-four squads and around 380 places across U6 to U16.

The new teams

  • U9 Pumas (boys) — the second U9 squad. Last season's U8 intake was our biggest ever and we promised no waiting list would follow them up. Kept.
  • U8 Robins (girls) — a new entry point to the girls' pathway, feeding the Swifts, Comets, Storm and Phoenix above them.
  • U10 Swifts development squad — for the Wildcats girls who want weekly training before committing to league football.

How places are allocated

Same rules as always, published so nobody has to wonder:

  1. Returning players keep their place automatically — nothing to do except renew before 31 July.
  2. Siblings of current players come next, in registration order.
  3. Everyone else in registration order — the form timestamp is the queue, no exceptions and no "a word with the coach" shortcuts.

Friendship requests still outrank squad balancing. If your child wants to play with their mate, say so on the form and we will make it work.

No Millbrook kid sits on a waiting list while a neighbouring squad has a space. If an age group fills up, we start another team. That's been the rule since 1998.

What it costs this season

Subs are unchanged for the fourth year running: £10 a month for U6–U7, £14 for U8–U12, £16 for U13–U16, kit included. The sibling discount and the hardship fund carry on exactly as before — one confidential email to the treasurer is all it takes.

Key dates

  • 31 July — renewal deadline for returning players
  • w/c 3 August — coaches phone every new registration
  • w/c 17 August — pre-season training starts, all age groups
  • Sun 6 September — first league fixtures

Register here — it takes five minutes, and the first month is free.
