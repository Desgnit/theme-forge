Joining Millbrook Juniors

Five minutes to register, first month free, and your child's coach calls before the first session. Here's everything parents ask about, up front.

How it works

Three steps to Saturday

  1. 1

    Register below

    Tell us your child's age, school year and whether they'd like the boys', girls' or mixed pathway — and if they have mates already here.

  2. 2

    Coach phones you

    Within a week you'll hear from the right age-group coach with a taster session invite. No forms at the pitch, no payment yet.

  3. 3

    Try a free month

    Four sessions before you pay a penny. If it's a yes, subs start and the kit bag comes out. If not — no hard feelings, ever.

Questions

Parents ask us

My child has never played — will they be behind?

No. Every squad mixes experience levels and coaches plan for it. Under-9s especially: half of every new intake has never played in a team.

Can my daughter play in a boys' team?

Yes — mixed football is FA-permitted through U16 and several girls play in our boys' squads. We'll lay out both options and she picks.

What if their friends are in a different squad?

Tell us on the form — friendship requests outrank ability balancing at this club, and squads within an age group are deliberately not streamed by ability.

Do parents have to stay during sessions?

U6–U9: yes, stay on site. U10 up: your call, as long as pick-up is on time. The clubhouse does a decent coffee either way.

Mid-season joining — allowed?

Yes, any month September to May, subs pro-rated. League registration takes about a week; training can start immediately.
