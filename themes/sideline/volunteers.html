Volunteer with us

Forty-two volunteers run this club and we need about six more every season, because kids keep having birthdays. You don't need to know football — you need two hours a week.

Pick your job

Roles we always need

Team coach

~3 hrs/week · badges funded

Run training and matchdays with a co-coach — nobody coaches alone here. We fund your FA Introduction to Coaching, DBS and first aid, and pair you with a mentor for season one.

Assistant / parent helper

~1–2 hrs/week

Cones out, bibs sorted, drills demoed badly to make the kids laugh. The classic route in — half our head coaches started exactly here.

Welfare officer

~2 hrs/week · training funded

The most important job at the club. FA Safeguarding and Welfare Officer courses funded; you're the person every parent and kid can go to.

Young referee (14+)

Paid per match

We fund the FA refereeing course for over-14s who ref our mini soccer. Pocket money, presence, and the sideline code protects you — see it in action.

Club admin & registrar

~2 hrs/week, from home

Player registrations, league paperwork, kit orders. Suits someone organised who'd rather help from a laptop than a rainy touchline.

Matchday crew

Rota, ~1 shift/month

Tea hut, car park, pitch setup, respect barriers. One Sunday shift a month keeps sixty matches a season running on time.

Every volunteer is checked and supported

DBS checks and FA Safeguarding for every adult role, induction with the welfare team, and no volunteer is ever alone with children. Costs covered by the club, always.

Offer an hour

"I'm not a football person" is fine

Our treasurer has never watched a full match. Our best welfare officer thought offside was a rugby thing. Come anyway.
