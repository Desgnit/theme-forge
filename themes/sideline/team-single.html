U10 Swifts

Founded in 2023 when eight girls turned up to a Wildcats taster and refused to go home. Two seasons on, the Swifts run a squad of fourteen, play 7v7 in the Greenway Girls League, and have the loudest sideline at Brook Field by some distance.

What to bring

  • Shin pads — non-negotiable, training and matches
  • Water bottle, named · layers in winter
  • Boots for grass, trainers or astros for the 3G
  • Match kit is provided and washed on rota

Sideline code

Cheer every kid on both teams. Leave the ref alone — most of ours are 14-year-olds doing their first job. Coaching happens from the coach, encouragement from everyone else. It works: come and hear it.
