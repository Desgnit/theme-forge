How we do things

Three promises to every parent

1

Everyone plays

Every registered child gets at least half a game, every week, in every team — league position irrelevant. It's club policy and coaches are held to it.

2

Safe, qualified, checked

Every coach holds an FA qualification, a current DBS check and first-aid training. Our welfare officers attend sessions unannounced — that's the point.

3

Cost is never the barrier

Subs are £14 a month with kit included, siblings discounted, and a confidential hardship fund. No Millbrook kid has ever stopped playing over money.

Run by parents, for kids

No volunteers, no club. Simple as that.

Every coach, linesman and registrar at Millbrook is a volunteer — most started as a parent on the sideline. We fund your FA coaching badges, DBS and first-aid course; you give a couple of hours a week.

  • FA Playmaker & Introduction to Coaching — fully funded
  • Non-coaching roles too: admin, welfare, fundraising, tea hut
  • Full induction and a mentor for your first season
