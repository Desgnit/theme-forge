From splash to swagger

All services & prices

Full groom

Bath, fluff dry, full clip or scissor styling to your dog's shape, plus nails, ears and a finishing spritz. From £38 for small dogs.

Bath & tidy

The in-between groom — bath, blow dry, thorough brush-out and a tidy of face, feet and feathers to keep the coat manageable.

Puppy introduction sessions

Two gentle half-hour visits: sniff the salon, hear the dryer, have paws handled, eat an unreasonable number of treats. Lifelong good grooming starts here.

Hand stripping

Traditional hand strip for wiry coats — border terriers, schnauzers, wire fox terriers — keeping the colour and texture clippers would ruin.

De-shedding treatment

Double-coat soak, conditioning, high-velocity dry and rake-out. The undercoat leaves with us — not on your sofa, car and cardigans.

Nail trims & teeth

Quick nail trims between grooms and gentle teeth cleaning as an add-on. Clicky claws on the kitchen floor? Ten minutes and done.
