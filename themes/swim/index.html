Lakes & prices

Willow Pool

Our original lake — 24 comfortable pegs and carp to 28lb, with a good head of doubles that feed all year. The one most regulars head for first.

Kingfisher Pool

Mixed coarse water full of tench, bream and roach — bites all day on simple tactics, which makes it perfect for youngsters and newcomers.

Specimen Lake

Our big-fish water with carp to 35lb. Members and night syndicate only — there's a waiting list, and it moves quicker than you'd think.

Opening times

Dawn till dusk, every day of the year bar Christmas Day. Gates open half an hour before first light; last tickets sold two hours before dusk.

Tackle shop

On-site shop at the lodge with bait, hooks, line and the bits you forgot — plus hot drinks, bacon rolls at weekends and honest advice for free.

Facilities

Clean toilets by the car park, hard paths to the water, and purpose-built disabled pegs on all three lakes. Dogs on leads are welcome.
