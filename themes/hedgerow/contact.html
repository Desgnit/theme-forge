Before you set off

When exactly are you open?

Shop and butchery: Tuesday to Saturday 9–5, Sunday 10–4, closed Mondays. The café kitchen closes at 3. The milk vending machine and egg shed run every day, 7am to 7pm — Mondays included.

Can I order ahead and collect?

Please do — it's how the regulars work. Ring or send the form and we'll have it boxed at the counter under your name. Beef boxes need a week's notice; most counter orders need a day.

Is the raw milk really unpasteurised?

Yes — straight from the morning milking, chilled fast, tested to the letter of our licence and sold within a day. It tastes of the season the cows are eating, which is rather the point.

Can I bring the dog?

On a lead, gladly — the yard, the outside tables and the water bowl by the door are all theirs. Food rules keep them out of the shop itself, assistance dogs excepted.
