What's on

Classes

Full timetable

Boxing fundamentals

Stance, footwork, the jab and how to hold yourself. Where every single member starts — total beginners are the point, not the exception.

Sparring (invite only)

Controlled, coached and matched by the coaches — you'll be invited when you're ready, never pushed. Headguards and gumshields, always.

Juniors — 8–11 & 12–16

Discipline, fitness and confidence in two age groups, run to England Boxing safeguarding standards. No contact until they're ready and you say so.

Ladies-only session

Same proper coaching, women-only room, coached by Dani. Half the class came "just to try it" and stayed for years.

Boxfit & conditioning

Bags, pads, ropes and circuits — all the training, none of the contact. The busiest lunchtime hour in Salford.

1-to-1 pad work

Private sessions with a coach, booked around your week. Technique, fitness or bout preparation — you set the goal.
