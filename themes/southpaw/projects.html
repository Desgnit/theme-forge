Our fighters

Every result on this page started with a nervous first session. Most of our members never compete — but for the ones who want it, this is the pathway, and the whole club turns out on show night.

The first-bout story, every time

It goes like this: a free first session, months of fundamentals, an invite to sparring, then one day your name on the show poster. No one is rushed, no one is thrown in — and around nine in ten members never compete at all. Both paths are the club.
