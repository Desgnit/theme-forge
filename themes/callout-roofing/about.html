Our story

The photo-first roofers

Gary started Ridgeline in 2004 with one rule born from watching cowboys work: show the customer the roof. Every survey since has come with photos or drone footage of the actual problem — no photos, no invoice.

That one rule built a five-man firm with a 4.9 rating and a waiting list every autumn. Turns out people can spot honesty from the ground, even if they can't spot a slipped tile.
