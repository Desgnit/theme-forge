The juniors

Where the first team starts

Seven of last season's first-team squad came through our juniors. Sessions run all year on the training paddock behind the Foundry Ground sticks, with pathways for boys and girls from U8s to U16s — and nobody is turned away over subs.

  • RFL-qualified coaches, all DBS-checked and first-aid trained
  • Girls' pathway with dedicated U12s, U14s and U16s squads
  • Open sign-on every July — no scouting, no politics, just rugby league
