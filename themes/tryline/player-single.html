No. 7 · Scrum-half

Danny Lofthouse

Signed from Castleton for a crate of the sponsor's best in 2021 — now the sharpest half in the division and the first name on the team sheet. Fourteen tries and 89 goals last season, including the lot in the derby, and somehow still turns up early to help pad the posts.

Signature moment

The last-minute penalty against Saltford in the 2021 Challenge Trophy final — struck from the touchline, into the wind, over the same sticks his grandad stood behind for forty years.
