The father-and-daughter team behind the van

One blue van, two Cavells, and every window on the round known by name since 2012.

Our story

Started with a bucket and Dad's old ladder

Steve picked up his first round in 2012 — thirty houses, a bucket, a squeegee and a borrowed ladder. In 2015 he swapped the ladder for a pure-water pole system: safer, kinder to flower beds, and it reaches third-storey glass that a ladder never could. His daughter Holly joined full-time in 2019 and dragged the round into this century — the night-before texts, online payments and the tidy little schedule that means we're in your street every four weeks, on the dot.

The eco bit isn't a slogan, it's just how pure water works: rainwater filtered and deionised in the van, no detergents, no chemicals — safe for pets, ponds and paintwork, and glass actually stays cleaner for longer because there's no soapy residue for dirt to cling to.
