Common questions

Do you sell to the public?

We're a trade supplier, but the counter serves anyone — you'll just pay counter price rather than account price.

How fast can I open a credit account?

Usually same day. Bring or email your company details and two trade references; most applications clear within hours.

Can you deliver direct to site?

Yes — booked slots, tail-lift vehicles and phased call-off against project orders. Tell us the site constraints when you order.

What's your returns policy?

Unused stocked lines back within 30 days in original packaging for full credit. Specials and cut cable are non-returnable — we'll always flag those before you commit.
