Skip, Rink 1 · Club captain

Ray Donnelly

Forty-one years a member, seven times club singles champion, and still the first name on the Saturday card. Ray joined in 1985 after "one go" at a works outing and has since skipped Rink 1 through four league titles, taught half the newcomers' roll-up their first draw shot, and never once let a wood be lifted before the green's been walked.

Signature moment

The last wood of the 2024 county pennant final: two down, one end to play, and Ray drew shot to within a hand's width of the jack. The photograph of the measure hangs over the clubhouse fireplace.

Off the green
