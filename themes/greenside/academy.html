New to bowls? Start here.

Free coaching taster sessions on the flat green, all summer long. We lend you the bowls, the coaching is unhurried, and the only thing you need to bring is a pair of flat-soled shoes.

How it works

Gentle coaching, no hurry, no fuss

A taster session lasts about ninety minutes. One of our qualified coaches sets you up with a set of club bowls the right size for your hand, shows you the delivery, and lets you get the feel of the green at your own pace. By the second visit most people are playing short ends; by the fourth, most people are hooked.

Your first four sessions are completely free, and there's no obligation afterwards. If you do join, playing membership is £60 a season — less than the price of a coffee a week — and the club lends new members a set of woods for as long as they need them.

  • Bowls England qualified coaches at every session
  • All equipment loaned free — bowls, pushers, measures, the lot
  • A third of last year's newcomers now play for a club team

Questions

People ask us

I've never bowled — will I be out of my depth?

Not for a moment. Taster sessions are only for beginners, the coaching is one-to-one or in pairs, and nobody keeps score. Most of our members started exactly where you are.

What should I wear?

Flat-soled shoes — ordinary trainers are perfect — and comfortable clothes. The green is precious, so heels and studs stay off it; everything else is entirely up to you.

Do I need my own bowls?

No — and please don't buy any yet. The club lends sets in every size and weight, and once you know what suits your hand a coach will help you choose, second-hand or new, without spending a fortune.

What happens after the free sessions?

Entirely up to you. Many join the newcomers' roll-up (£2 a session including tea), and playing membership is £60 a season if you want more. There's no pressure and no hard sell — the kettle's on either way.

Bring a friend

Tasters are more fun in twos — most of our newest members came with a neighbour, a spouse or the person from three doors down. Book together and we'll put you on the same rink.
