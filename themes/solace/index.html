Our services

All services

Traditional funerals

A full funeral service, shaped around the person and your family's wishes. We take care of every detail, from the first call to the day itself.

Cremation services

Attended services at Stonefall and the surrounding crematoria, arranged with the same care and attention as any funeral.

Direct cremation

A simple cremation without a service, carried out with dignity. Our lowest-cost choice, explained plainly, with nothing added.

Green & woodland burials

Natural burial grounds in the countryside near Harrogate, with coffins of willow, bamboo and other sustainable materials.

Pre-paid funeral plans

Record your wishes and settle the cost at today's prices. Regulated plans, explained without pressure, in your own time.

Repatriation

Bringing someone home to Yorkshire, or arranging their journey overseas. We handle the paperwork and the practicalities on your behalf.
