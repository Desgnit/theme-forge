Our services

However you choose to say goodbye, we will arrange it with care, and explain the costs plainly along the way. If you are not sure what is right, talk to us. There is no obligation, and no hurry.

Traditional funerals

A full funeral service, shaped around the person and your family's wishes — church, graveside or elsewhere. We take care of every detail, from the first call to the day itself.

Cremation services

Attended services at Stonefall and the surrounding crematoria, arranged with the same care and attention as any funeral, with music, readings and tributes as you wish.

Direct cremation

A simple cremation without a service, carried out with the same dignity as every funeral in our care. It is our lowest-cost choice, and we will say so plainly — from £1,295, with nothing added.

Green & woodland burials

Natural burial grounds in the countryside near Harrogate, with coffins of willow, bamboo, cardboard and other sustainable materials, and a tree or wildflowers in place of a headstone.

Pre-paid funeral plans

Record your wishes and settle the cost at today's prices, sparing your family decisions later. Our plans are regulated, and explained without pressure, in your own time.

Repatriation

Bringing someone home to Yorkshire, or arranging their journey overseas. We handle the consular paperwork, the carriers and the practicalities on your behalf.

What to expect

From your first call onwards

  1. 1

    Your first call

    Our line is answered at every hour by a member of our own team, never a call centre. We will listen, and take care of whatever is needed straight away.

  2. 2

    Into our care

    We bring the person who has died into our care, day or night, and look after them at our private chapel of rest on Cold Bath Road.

  3. 3

    Arranging together

    We meet when you are ready, at our home or yours, and shape the funeral around the person. You will have the costs in writing before anything is confirmed.

  4. 4

    The day, and after

    We guide everyone gently through the day itself. Afterwards, we remain here for you — for memorials, paperwork, or simply a conversation.

Unsure what is right for your family?

Talk it through with us, with no obligation. Our fees are explained plainly, and our full standardised price list — required of all UK funeral directors by the CMA — is available at any time.
