Four generations of quiet care

Founded in 1932 and still owned by the same family, we have served Harrogate through more than ninety years of change — with the same steadiness throughout.

Our history

Serving Harrogate since 1932

Albert Fairbourne opened our doors on Cold Bath Road in 1932, believing that a funeral should be arranged with patience, honesty and no fuss. His great-granddaughter, Eleanor Fairbourne, leads the firm today — the fourth generation of the family to do so.

Much has changed in ninety years. Our way of working has not. We remain independent, we answer our own telephone at every hour, and we care for every person at our own premises, from the first call to the final farewell.

Who and where

The team, the house, the town

A small, familiar team

Eleanor Fairbourne leads a team of six, several of whom have been with us for decades. Whoever answers the telephone will know your name and your arrangement.

Our chapel of rest

Our home on Cold Bath Road includes a private chapel of rest, quiet arrangement rooms and level access throughout. You are welcome to visit your loved one during office hours, and at other times by arrangement.

Part of Harrogate life

We support the town's hospices and bereavement groups, hold an annual memorial service each December, and keep a book of remembrance for every family we have served.
