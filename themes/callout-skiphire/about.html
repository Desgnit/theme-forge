The people behind the lorries

One family, five lorries, and twenty-eight years of Doncaster's clear-outs, rip-outs and building sites.

Our story

Started with one flatbed and a dozen skips

Ray Marsden bought his first skip lorry in 1998 and spent the next decade learning every drive, ginnel and weight limit in Doncaster. His daughter Kelly runs the office now, Ray still drives on Saturdays, and the plan has never changed — turn up when you said, charge what you quoted.

Twenty-eight years on, most of our work comes from repeat customers and builders who've had us on speed dial for years. The lorries are washed, the paperwork is same-day, and the driver still rings twenty minutes out.
