Lessons & prices

Every lesson is one-to-one, in a dual-controlled car, taught calmly and at your pace. £38 an hour, £350 for a block of ten, and no pressure to buy more hours than you need. Not sure where to start? Call and we'll advise honestly.

Standard lessons (manual)

£38/hr or 10 hours for £350. One-to-one in our manual Ford Fiesta, following the DVSA syllabus with a progress record you can see after every lesson.

Automatic lessons

No clutch, no stalling — just steering, observation and road sense. Perfect for nervous learners and anyone who wants to pass sooner. Same prices as manual.

Intensive & semi-intensive

From £680. Twenty to forty hours over two to six weeks, planned backwards from your test date. Semi-intensive spreads the same hours around work or college.

Refresher lessons

Passed years ago but stopped driving? A few calm hours to rebuild confidence — city traffic, parking, motorways — with no judgement about how long it's been.

Pass Plus

The official six-module course for new drivers: motorways, night driving, all-weather and rural roads. Some insurers discount your premium when you complete it.

Theory test support

A revision plan, hazard-perception practice and mock questions woven into your lessons — so the theory pass lands right when you need it to book your practical.

How lessons work

First lesson to full licence, in four steps

  1. 1

    Your first lesson

    We start on quiet roads near your home. You'll drive within the first ten minutes — gently, with dual controls as the safety net.

  2. 2

    A plan you can see

    An honest estimate of the hours you'll need and a progress record updated every lesson — so there are no surprises and no padding.

  3. 3

    Build up, calmly

    From quiet estates to town centres, dual carriageways and the real test routes — each step only when you're ready for it.

  4. 4

    Mock tests & test day

    Full mock tests at Wigston or Cannock Street before the real thing, then you sit your test in the car you learned in.
