Theory to test: your timeline

Most learners: pass the theory first (2–4 weeks of app practice — we'll help), then book the practical, where waits are often 8–12 weeks. That wait is your friend: we plan lessons backwards from test day so you peak at the right moment. Typical learner: 30–40 hours over 4–6 months. In a hurry? An intensive course compresses it to 2–4 weeks.
