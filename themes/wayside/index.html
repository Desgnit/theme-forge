Pitches & prices

Full prices & seasons

Grass tent pitch

A generous patch of well-drained meadow for tents up to six berth. Park beside your pitch, fall asleep to the sea. from £18/night

Electric hook-up pitch

Grass pitch with a 10-amp electric hook-up for tents, trailer tents and campervans. Kettle on, phones charged. £26/night

Hardstanding pitch

Level gravel hardstanding with 16-amp hook-up for caravans and motorhomes — no soggy pitching in April, no ruts in October. £28/night

Seasonal pitch

Leave the caravan with us from March to October and come and go as you please. A handful come up each year. from £1,450/season

Awnings, pups & visitors

Awnings and pup tents on your pitch are free within your pitch boundary. Dogs £2 a night (max two), day visitors £3 — just sign them in at reception.

Hikers & cyclists

Walking the Cleveland Way or cycling the Cinder Track? Small-tent walker rate, no booking needed most weeknights. £12/night
