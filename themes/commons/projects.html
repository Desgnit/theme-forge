What's on

Something for every age, every week of the year. New faces are always welcome at every club below — just turn up, or contact the organiser via the noticeboard in the lobby.

Coming up: one-off events

The Elmsworth Summer Fete (second Saturday in July, on the green and in the hall), the harvest supper in October, the charity quiz night each November and the village panto every January. Details go up on the lobby noticeboard and the parish newsletter first.
