Find us & parking

The Green, Elmsworth, Gloucestershire GL99 0AA — next to the church, opposite the shop. Free car park for about 30 cars behind the hall, with step-free access and reserved accessible spaces by the door. Please don't park on the lane; overflow parking on the green verge can be arranged for bigger events.

Clubs & regular hirers

Weekly classes and monthly clubs get a discounted block-booking rate, a set of keys of your own once you're established, and first refusal on your regular slot each term.
