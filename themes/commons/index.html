Our facilities

Hire details & rates

Main hall — seats 120

A light, sprung-floored hall seating 120 (150 standing), with tables and chairs included. Big enough for a ceilidh, cosy enough for a coffee morning.

Committee room

A quieter room for up to 20 — ideal for meetings, small classes, book groups and the parish council. Available on its own or alongside the hall.

Kitchen

Fully fitted with a range cooker, fridge, urn, crockery and cutlery for 120. Serving hatch straight into the main hall — teas and buffets made easy.

Stage

A proper raised stage with curtains and lighting bar — home to the panto, school shows and the monthly film night's silver screen.

Car park

Free parking for around 30 cars behind the hall, with overflow on the green verge by arrangement for bigger events.

Accessible to everyone

Step-free entrance, wheelchair-accessible toilet, hearing loop in the main hall and reserved parking by the door. Just ask if you need anything else.
