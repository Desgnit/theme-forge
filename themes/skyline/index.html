The academy

Where the first team starts

Six of last season's first-team squad came through our academy. Sessions run all year on the 4G grids at Harbour Park, with pathways for boys and girls from U7 to U18 — and nobody is turned away over subs.

  • FA-qualified coaches, all DBS-checked and first-aid trained
  • Girls' pathway with dedicated U10, U13 and U16 squads
  • Open trials every July — no scouting, no politics, just football
