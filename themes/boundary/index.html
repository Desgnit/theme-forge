More than the 1st XI

9 teams, one badge

Saturday XIs

Three league sides every Saturday. The 1st XI were runners-up in WVL Division One last season — the closest title race in a generation.

Women's & Sunday XI

Women's softball champions and a Sunday friendly XI that takes anyone. Nets on Wednesdays — new players always welcome.

Juniors

All Stars, Dynamos and hardball squads at U13 and U15, ECB-qualified coaches, and a clear route to the 1st XI.

Junior cricket

Where the 1st XI starts

Six of last season's 1st XI came through our junior section. Coaching nights run all summer at The Meadow, with All Stars for ages 5–8, Dynamos for 8–11 and hardball squads at U13 and U15 — and nobody is turned away over subs.

  • ECB-qualified coaches, all DBS-checked and first-aid trained
  • Girls' pathway with dedicated Dynamos and U13 groups
  • Taster sessions every May — no trials, no politics, just cricket
