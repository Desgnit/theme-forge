Services

Every scaffold below comes with a fixed written quote, a TG20:21 compliant design, and a handover certificate before anyone climbs it. Not sure what you need? Send a photo of the elevation — we'll tell you straight.

Residential scaffolding

Re-roofs, extensions, chimneys and render — erected around your garden, not through it, and dismantled the day your trades finish.

Commercial contracts

New-builds, refurbs and phased contracts with TG20:21 compliant designs, RAMS before day one, and one gaffer to call for the whole hire.

Temporary roofs

Weatherproof tin-sheet and shrink-wrapped roofs so the re-roof or restoration carries on whatever the sky is doing. Wind-load calcs included.

Edge protection

Guardrails, toe boards and brick guards to current standards for roof work and flat-roof plant jobs — up fast, signed off, kept inspected.

Scaffold towers

Static and mobile towers for gutter runs, soffits and short jobs — delivered, erected and collected. Small hires welcome, priced fairly.

Inspections & handover certificates

Handover certificates on every scaffold, statutory 7-day inspections through the hire, and post-storm checks — tagged, logged, emailed same day.

How it works

Quote to dismantle, in four steps

  1. 1

    Tell us the job

    Call, message or use the form — photos of the elevation help us price accurately first time.

  2. 2

    Free site survey & fixed quote

    We measure up, check the ground and access, and put the price in writing. The price is the price.

  3. 3

    We erect

    On the day we agreed, CISRS-carded crew, wagon loaded the night before — most residential scaffolds are up in a day.

  4. 4

    Inspect, certify, dismantle

    Handover certificate before anyone climbs it, 7-day inspections through the hire, then down and away the day you're done.
