Matchday directions

  • By car: free parking on Foundry Lane; overflow at the primary school on big matchdays.
  • By train: Redbrook station, then ten minutes on foot — follow the claret scarves.
  • Admission: adults £6, concessions £3, under-16s free with a paying adult. Cash or card at the gate.
