Services

Everything below comes fully insured — goods in transit to £20,000 — with fuel and mileage already in the quote, and the blankets, straps and trolleys on the van. Not sure which you need? Describe the load and we'll tell you straight.

Man & van

One mover, one Luton, from £45/hr. Single items, eBay and Facebook Marketplace pickups, and the sofa the seller swears "comes apart" — we bring the straps and the tape measure either way.

Flat & house moves

Studio to four-bed, from £340 fixed. Two Lutons run in relay so your old place empties while the new one fills, beds rebuilt and boxes in the rooms they're labelled for.

Office moves

Evenings and weekends so nobody loses a working day. Desks labelled, crates numbered, cables bagged and taped to the desk they belong to. IT boxed last, unboxed first.

Packing service

We arrive the day before with boxes, paper, bubble wrap and tape, and pack a room in about an hour. Plates arrive as plates. Materials included in the price.

Student moves

Halls to house share, house share back home for summer. Small loads priced kindly, and yes — we've moved a full drum kit up three flights before. Twice.

Waste-run add-on

Anything not making the cut goes to the tip or a charity while the van's already out. Licensed waste carrier, transfer notes kept, no fly-tipped mattress with your name on it.

How it works

Quote to keys, in four steps

  1. 1

    Tell us the move

    Form, call or message — postcodes, rough date, and whether there's a lift involved.

  2. 2

    Video survey by WhatsApp

    Walk us round your place on a ten-minute video call. No stranger with a clipboard in your hallway.

  3. 3

    Fixed quote, same day

    Crew size, van count, fuel and mileage — all in one number that doesn't move on the day.

  4. 4

    Moving day

    We arrive on time with everything wrapped, strapped and listed. You hold the kettle and point.
