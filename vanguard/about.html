Our story

Started with one borrowed van

Marcus started Vanguard in 2015 with a Transit borrowed from his uncle and a Saturday of moving favours for friends. The favours got reviews, the reviews got bookings, and the borrowed van eventually went home — replaced, in time, by two Lutons and a crew of four: Marcus, Dee, Tomasz and Rob, who between them have carried roughly every object Reading owns up or down a staircase.

A decade on, the method hasn't changed: turn up when we said, wrap everything like it's ours, and put the number in writing before the engine starts. About six in ten moves come from people we've moved before — or their mates, or their mums.
