Sam Harradine

Signed from Harnham Wanderers for a crate of the sponsor's best in 2022 — now the most feared all-rounder in the division and the first name on the team sheet. Seven hundred and forty-three league runs this season, including a hundred in the derby, and somehow still turns up early to help pull the covers.

The unbeaten 94 against Ashcombe in the 2023 Village Cup final — the winning boundary lofted over the same rope his grandad rolled flat every April for forty years.
