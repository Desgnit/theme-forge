Our story

From one counter in Leeds to national project supply

Calder Industrial started in 1987 as a trade counter selling cable and fittings to Yorkshire electricians. The counter is still there — but behind it now sits a 60,000 sq ft distribution centre, an in-house lighting design team and a stocked range of over 4,000 lines.

We stayed independent on purpose. It means we pick ranges on quality and availability rather than group buying deals, and when a delivery has to hit a site window, the person making that call is in the same building as the stock.

How we work

Three promises we're held to daily

01

Stock means stock

If the site shows it in stock, it's on the shelf — not on a container. Our 98.6% on-time-in-full rate is measured against the date we promised, not the date we managed.

02

Answers, not order takers

Our internal sales team are trained on the products they sell. Ask about emergency durations, driver compatibility or cable calcs and you'll get an answer, not a call-back.

03

Paperwork sorted

Datasheets, DoCs, photometric files and O&M pack contents come with the order — because chasing documents at handover costs you more than the fittings did.

Milestones

A short history

  1. 1987

    Trade counter opens in Leeds

    Two staff, one van, and a promise to have cable on the counter before the kettle boiled.

  2. 2003

    First distribution warehouse

    Move to Aire Valley Trade Park triples stockholding and starts nationwide next-day delivery.

  3. 2014

    Own-brand LED range launches

    The Apex, Vulcan and Sentinel families arrive — specified, tested and warrantied by us.

  4. 2019

    In-house lighting design team

    Relux and DIALux layouts, emergency schemes and payback reports offered free on project enquiries.

  5. 2024

    Full digital spec library

    LDT/IES photometry and BIM objects published for every stocked luminaire.
