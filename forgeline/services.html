Services

Distribution is the easy part. These are the services that keep contractors coming back — most of them free with a project order.

Lighting design & layouts

Send drawings in PDF or DWG and get a Relux/DIALux layout back — lux plots, uniformity, luminaire schedule and a priced kit list. Free with project enquiries, usually within 48 hours.

Emergency lighting compliance

Escape-route and open-area schemes designed to BS 5266-1, with spacing tables and test-key positions marked. We'll flag where your existing layout falls short before the fire officer does.

Project supply & call-off

Fixed pricing held for the programme, stock reserved against your order, and phased deliveries kitted per floor or area. Measured OTIF on every project — currently 98.6%.

Technical files on demand

Datasheets, LDT/IES photometry, BIM objects and Declarations of Conformity for every stocked luminaire — plus complete O&M pack contents assembled for handover.

Trade accounts & credit

30-day credit accounts opened in minutes with instant online statements. Project pricing and volume breaks applied automatically at 10, 50 and 100+ units.

Value engineering

Send the specified schedule and we'll price compliant equals line by line — same photometrics, same warranty, documented deviations — often 15–30% under the spec'd brand.

How a project runs

Drawing to delivery in four steps

  1. 1

    Send the drawings

    PDF, DWG or a phone photo of the plan — plus the activity in each area so we design to the right lux levels.

  2. 2

    Design & quote

    Compliant layout, energy comparison and a fixed-price kit list back within 48 hours. Revisions are free.

  3. 3

    Kitted delivery

    Stock reserved, then called off in phases — boxed and labelled per area, delivered to site on booked slots.

  4. 4

    Handover pack

    O&M contents, DoCs and warranty certificates issued at practical completion. Aftercare on one number.
