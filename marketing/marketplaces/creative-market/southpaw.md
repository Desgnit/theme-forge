## Excerpt (short description)

A dark, punchy website for a boxing gym — class timetable, no-contract membership pricing, coach profiles and fighter stories, with Anton display type and fight-red accents.

## Description

The demo gym (Southside Boxing Academy, Salford, England Boxing affiliated) is written to get someone through the door for their first session.

- **Classes & Timetable**: fundamentals, sparring, juniors, ladies-only, boxfit and 1-to-1 pads — each with its weekly slot
- Membership pricing block: monthly, junior and drop-in rates, no contracts
- **Our Fighters** page: amateur bout results, show nights and a first-bout story
- The head coach's story and the railway-arch gym
- First-session booking form with experience-level dropdown
- Its own design identity: near-black sections, oversized Anton headings, red slash accents
- 5 pages, HTML + compiled Tailwind CSS, no build step, vanilla JS, WCAG-AA, iPhone-tested
