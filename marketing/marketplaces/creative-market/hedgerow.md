## Description

Farm shops sell provenance, and Hedgerow is built to show it. The demo (Hedgerow Farm Shop, Peak District, farming since 1946) makes you hungry — which is the job.

- **The Shop**: filterable grid across butchery, greengrocery, dairy & eggs, bakery, pantry and hampers, with honest prices
- Product page built for provenance: breed, hanging time and what's in the box instead of tech specs
- Veg box delivery rounds, hampers and corporate gifts, counter orders, café corner
- Farm notes blog: lambing diary, why asparagus season is short
- Opening hours prominent everywhere; four generations of story without buzzword soup
- Its own design identity: Fraunces serif, cream paper tints, kraft-label price tags, offset hand-drawn borders
- 8 pages, HTML + compiled Tailwind CSS, no build step, vanilla JS, WCAG-AA, iPhone-tested
