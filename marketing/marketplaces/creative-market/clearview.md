## Description

Window cleaning runs on rounds, and Clearview is the rare template that understands that. The demo business (Clearview Window Cleaning, Norwich) sells reliability, not one-off jobs.

- Homepage built around the recurring round: 4- or 8-weekly visits, reminder texts, pay-online-after-each-clean
- Services: domestic rounds, conservatory roofs, gutter clearing with camera proof, fascias, solar panels, storefronts
- Round areas with postcodes, price guide by property size, reviews about turning up when promised
- Join-the-round form with frequency and property-type dropdowns
- Its own design identity: sky gradients, Nunito Sans, pill buttons, soft shadows — clean-glass airy
- 5 pages, HTML + compiled Tailwind CSS, no build step, vanilla JS, WCAG-AA, iPhone-tested
