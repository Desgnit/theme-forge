## Excerpt (short description)

An editorial Shopify OS 2.0 theme for fitness gear and training apparel — bone-white magazine layouts, ink-border product cards, an asymmetric lookbook grid and split heroes, on the same no-apps conversion engine.

## Description

Torque sells gear the way a good print catalogue does: big photography, confident type, nothing fussy.

- **Signature sections**: an asymmetric editorial lookbook grid and a split hero (huge condensed type one side, full-bleed image the other)
- Magazine details throughout: index rule-lines with small-caps labels, outlined display numerals ("01 / GEAR"), ink-bordered product cards, signal-orange highlights
- The full conversion kit from our Anvil engine: mega menu with images, quick view, slide-out AJAX cart with free-shipping bar, sticky add-to-cart, storefront filtering
- Bone-white surfaces, ink black, signal orange; Archivo display type up to poster weights
- 28 JSON-configurable sections; every colour and font editable in theme settings
- Speed-first vanilla JavaScript, accessible drawers and modals; 100% original code, sellable on any marketplace

Install: Online Store → Themes → Upload zip. Full customisation guide in the README.
