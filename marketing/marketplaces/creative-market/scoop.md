## Description

Scoop is built for protein powders, pre-workouts and supplement lines — dark, precise and loud in the right places.

- **Signature sections**: a scrolling claims marquee ("LAB TESTED • 27G PROTEIN • ZERO JUNK") and a nutrition-label-styled benefits panel with mono numerals and thick rules
- The full conversion kit inherited from our Anvil engine: mega menu with image tiles, quick-view modals, slide-out AJAX cart with free-shipping progress bar, sticky add-to-cart, sale badges, storefront filtering
- Near-black surfaces with acid-volt accents; Space Grotesk display type at poster sizes
- 28 JSON-configurable sections; colours, typography, radius and layout width all editable in theme settings
- Speed-first vanilla JavaScript — no jQuery, no frameworks; accessible drawers and modals
- 100% original code, licensed for sale on any marketplace
