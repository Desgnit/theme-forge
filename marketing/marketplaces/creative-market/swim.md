## Excerpt (short description)

A complete website for a day-ticket fishery — lakes and prices up front, catch reports, venue rules and records, in deep-water blue and reed green.

## Description

Anglers check three things before they travel: prices, stock and rules. Swim puts all three on the surface — the demo venue (Willow Pool Fisheries, Shropshire, three lakes on a family farm since 1987) reads like somewhere you'd actually fish.

- **Lakes & Prices**: each lake with its stock, pegs and character (runs water, silvers lake, specimen syndicate), plus a day/evening/season ticket price table
- **Catch Reports** page: recent catches with weights, baits and pegs, and a venue records list
- Day-ticket prices and opening times on the homepage, above the fold
- Rules summary written like a fishery actually writes them (barbless only, unhooking mats, no braid)
- About page: the farm's story, stocking policy, otter fencing, match bookings
- 5 pages, HTML + compiled Tailwind CSS, no build step, vanilla JS, WCAG-AA, iPhone-tested
- Every colour is a CSS variable — five-minute rebrand, documented in the README

Perfect for: day-ticket fisheries, carp lakes, angling clubs, farm diversification ventures.
