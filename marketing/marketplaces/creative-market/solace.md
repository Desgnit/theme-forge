## Description

Most templates shout. Solace is quiet on purpose — designed for a family firm (the demo is Fairbourne & Sons, Harrogate, serving families since 1932) where the website's job is to reassure at the hardest moment.

- 24-hour phone line prominent on every page, with "what to do first" guidance written in plain, kind English
- Services explained honestly: traditional funerals, cremation, direct cremation, woodland burial, pre-paid plans, repatriation
- Transparent-pricing section aligned with the CMA's standardised price list requirement for UK funeral directors
- Testimonials page with believable, respectful words from families
- About page: four generations of history, the team, the chapel of rest
- 5 pages, HTML + compiled Tailwind CSS, no build step, vanilla JS, WCAG-AA, iPhone-tested
- A muted dusk-and-brass palette, changeable via CSS variables in minutes

Perfect for: independent funeral directors, family firms, memorial services.
