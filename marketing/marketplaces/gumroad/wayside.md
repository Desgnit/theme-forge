## Summary (one-liner)

A fresh-air website for a caravan and camping site — pitch types and seasonal prices up front, facilities and site rules in plain English, quiet-site policy, walks from the gate.

## Description

Campers compare pitches, prices and shower blocks — Wayside answers all three above the fold. The demo site (Wayside Meadow, North Yorkshire coast, 60 pitches, March–October) is family-run and proudly quiet.

- **Pitches & Prices**: grass, electric hook-up, hardstanding and seasonal pitches, each with size, amps and awning rules, priced by season
- Facilities strip: heated shower block, laundry, dog walk field, playground, shop with camping gas
- Arrival times, deposits and cancellation explained like a human wrote them
- The site & around: sea views, Cleveland Way walks, Whitby 15 minutes, gates locked 10pm
- Booking enquiry form with pitch type, dates, party size and dog flag — plus the narrow-lane sat-nav warning
- Its own design identity: sea teal and campfire orange, Bitter slab type, contour-pattern hero bands, badge price tags
- 5 pages, HTML + compiled Tailwind CSS, no build step, vanilla JS, WCAG-AA, iPhone-tested
