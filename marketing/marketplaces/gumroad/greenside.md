## Summary (one-liner)

A heritage-styled website for a bowls club — fixtures and rink results, membership and open days, new-bowler taster sessions, in bowling green and brass with readable serif elegance.

## Description

Thousands of UK bowls clubs, almost no templates made for them. Greenside's demo club (Avonford Bowls Club, est. 1907, two greens) is written for the actual audience: clear, welcoming, unhurried.

- Fixtures and results as bowls matches with rink scores, plus the league table
- **Members & Teams** page: Saturday league, midweek triples, ladies' team and the newcomers' roll-up
- **New Bowlers** page: free taster sessions, what to wear, equipment loaned, green etiquette
- Club history with county pennants, finals-day news, green fees and open days
- Heritage design of its own: Playfair Display serif, cream tints, double-rule borders, larger base text for readability
- 8 pages, HTML + compiled Tailwind CSS, no build step, vanilla JS, WCAG-AA, iPhone-tested
