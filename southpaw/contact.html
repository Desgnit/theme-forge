Find us

Arch 19, Frederick Road railway arches, Salford M6 — two minutes' walk from Langworthy tram stop, free parking on the cobbles outside. Look for the red door under the viaduct; if a train rumbles over mid-round, that's normal.

Parents & schools

Every juniors coach is DBS-checked and safeguarding trained, and parents are welcome at every session. Schools: we run six-week non-contact blocks in local primaries and secondaries — email us about the community programme.
