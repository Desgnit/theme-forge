Classes & timetable

Every class is coached to the person in front of us — complete beginners train next to carded boxers and both get better. Your first session is free, whichever one you pick.

Boxing fundamentals

Stance, guard, footwork and the four punches that everything else is built on. This is where every member starts — no fitness bar to clear first.

Mon & Wed 6:30–7:30pm · Sat 10–11am

Sparring (invite only)

Matched by a coach, supervised throughout, headguards and gumshields non-negotiable. You'll be invited when you're ready — most members never spar, and that's fine.

Tue & Thu 7:30–9pm

Juniors 8–11

Games, pads and non-contact skills work that builds discipline and confidence. Run to England Boxing safeguarding standards; parents welcome to watch.

Tue & Thu 5–5:45pm

Juniors 12–16

Proper technical boxing for teenagers, with a pathway to skills bouts and carded amateur boxing for those who want it — and no pressure for those who don't.

Tue & Thu 6–7pm

Ladies-only session

The full fundamentals session in a women-only room, coached by Dani. Same standards, same sweat — many members move into the mixed classes, plenty happily don't.

Fri 6–7pm

Boxfit & conditioning

Bags, pads, ropes and circuits with zero contact. Come in a lunch break, leave wrung out and grinning. All levels, every session scaled.

Mon, Wed & Fri 12:15–1pm

1-to-1 pad work

A coach, a ring and a plan built around you — technique tune-ups, bout preparation or just a private start if a class feels like too much on day one.

Membership

One price, no contracts

  1. £35

    Adults / month

    Every adult class, every week — fundamentals, boxfit, and sparring when invited. Cancel any time.

  2. £20

    Juniors / month

    Both weekly juniors sessions plus club show nights. Nobody's ever been turned away over money — talk to us.

  3. £8

    Drop-in

    Any single class, pay on the door. Handy for shift workers and the commitment-shy — no judgement either way.

  4. £0

    First session

    Always free, for everyone, in any class. Gloves and wraps provided — just bring water and trainers.
