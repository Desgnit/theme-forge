Packages

Four packages, listed line by line so you know exactly what you're paying for. Prices cover most cars; bigger, muddier or very special ones are priced honestly up front — the number we quote is the number you pay, and nobody upsells you on your own driveway.

Mini valet

£35 · about 1 hour

  • Two-bucket pH-neutral hand wash — never a single dirty sponge
  • Wheels, arches and tyres cleaned and dressed
  • Full interior vacuum, boot included
  • Dash, console and door cards wiped and finished matte
  • Glass cleaned inside and out, streak-free

Full valet

£70 · about 3 hours

  • Everything in the mini valet
  • Carpets and mats shampooed and extracted
  • Interior plastics deep-cleaned, vents brushed out
  • Leather cleaned and conditioned
  • Door shuts and sills degreased
  • Tar spot removal and a spray sealant to finish

Enhancement detail

from £180 · full day

  • Full valet included
  • Chemical decontamination — iron fallout and tar
  • Clay bar to a glass-smooth surface
  • Paint depth measured before a machine touches it
  • Single-stage machine polish — removes most swirl marks
  • Durable paint sealant, glass sealed too

Ceramic coating

from £450 · 1–2 days

  • Everything in the enhancement detail
  • Multi-stage machine correction as the paint needs
  • Panel wipe-down to bare, oil-free paint
  • Professional-grade ceramic coating, layered and cured
  • Years of protection — dirt sheds, water beads
  • Aftercare kit and a written wash guide included

Maintenance plans

How it works

Photos to finished, in four steps

  1. 1

    Send photos

    Message a couple of photos, your postcode and how bad it really is. Honesty gets you an accurate price first time.

  2. 2

    Fixed price & slot

    We confirm the price and a date, usually within the hour. Weekend slots go first every single week.

  3. 3

    We come to you

    Driveway, street or work car park — we bring water, power, lighting and every product. You bring nothing.

  4. 4

    Walk-round & aftercare

    We hand back with a walk-round in good light and honest aftercare advice, so the finish outlasts the weekend.
