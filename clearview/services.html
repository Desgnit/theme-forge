Services

Everything below is done with pure water, priced clearly before we start, and paid online after you've seen the results. Most of it can bolt straight onto your regular round.

Domestic window rounds

The heart of what we do. Every 4 weeks (most popular) or every 8 — glass, frames, sills and doors each visit. A 3-bed semi is from £15 a time, and you can skip any visit by text.

Conservatory roofs

Algae and lichen off the panels, finials and gullies included, light back in the room. From £40, or discounted when it's part of your round.

Gutter clearing

High-reach vacuum from the ground, camera on the pole, before-and-after footage texted to you as proof it's actually clear. From £60 per house.

Fascias, soffits & cladding

The white uPVC that slowly turns green. Brushed and rinsed with pure water — no bleach near your plants — so the whole front sparkles, not just the glass.

Solar panel cleaning

Deionised water and a soft brush only — no detergents, nothing to void a warranty. Dirty panels quietly lose output; most customers book spring and autumn.

Commercial & storefronts

Shopfronts, cafés, offices and showrooms — weekly or fortnightly, done before opening, one tidy invoice a month. Ask about our city-centre morning round.

How the round works

From first text to gleaming, in four steps

  1. 1

    Tell us your address

    Use the form or text us — we'll reply with your street's round day and a fixed price per visit.

  2. 2

    We slot you in

    Your street already has a day. First clean is usually within a fortnight — first visits take a little longer while we get frames back to standard.

  3. 3

    Text the night before

    So the gate's unlocked and the car's off the drive. Not convenient? Reply SKIP and we'll see you next time — no charge, no fuss.

  4. 4

    Pay online after

    Card, bank transfer or GoCardless once the clean is done. No cash under the mat, no standing at the door counting change.

Want your street on the round?

If a neighbour's already with us, we're on your street every four weeks anyway — joining takes one text and your first clean is usually within a fortnight.
