Price guide

Terraced from £12 · 3-bed semi from £15 · 4-bed detached from £20 per visit. Conservatory windows add £3. First cleans are usually 1.5× while we bring frames and sills back to standard — every visit after that is the regular price.

Which day is my street?

Every street on the round has its day — Thorpe St Andrew is Tuesdays, Taverham Thursdays, the city centre Fridays. Send your postcode and we'll text yours back, plus the date of our next visit.
