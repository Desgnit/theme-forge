The site & around

Eleven acres of clifftop meadow between the village and the sea. Here's what you're pitching on — and where your boots can take you before breakfast.

A quiet site, on purpose

No groups, no stag or hen parties, no music you can hear from the next pitch. Gates locked at 10pm, quiet until 7:30am. If you want a peaceful night under a big sky, you'll fit right in.
