Pitches & prices

Every price on this page is the whole price — pitch, two adults, hot showers, the lot. Arrive from 1pm, leave by 11am, and if plans change we're humans about it.

Grass tent pitch from £18/night

Roughly 10m × 9m of well-drained meadow, marked at the corners. Tents up to six berth, car parked alongside. No hook-up — that's rather the point.

Electric hook-up pitch £26/night

The same generous grass pitch with a 10-amp electric hook-up bollard. Suits tents, trailer tents and campervans. Bring a 25m cable and a UK site plug — the shop sells both if you forget.

Hardstanding pitch £28/night

Level gravel bays about 10m × 8m with 16-amp hook-up, for caravans and motorhomes up to 8m. Fresh water and grey-waste point within a hose length of every bay.

Seasonal pitch from £1,450/season

Your caravan on its own hardstanding from 1 March to 31 October, 16-amp hook-up and awning space included. A handful become available each winter — ask to join the list.

Awning rules, kept simple

One awning or one pup tent per pitch, free, as long as everything (including guy lines) stays inside your pitch markers. Breathable groundsheets only on grass — the meadow has to last all season.

Everything else

Extra adults £5, children (5–15) £3, under-5s free. Dogs £2 a night, max two, on leads except in the dog walk field. Hikers and cyclists with a small tent: £12, no booking needed most weeknights.

The practical bit

Booking, plainly

  1. 1

    Book & deposit

    One night's fee holds your pitch (25% for seasonals). Pay it by card over the phone or bank transfer — the rest when you arrive.

  2. 2

    Arrive from 1pm

    Check in at reception by 8pm please — the lane is no fun in the dark. Coming later? Ring ahead and we'll leave instructions.

  3. 3

    Leave by 11am

    Later departures are usually fine outside peak weeks — just ask the warden the day before. No charge for staying to walk the dog first.

  4. 4

    If plans change

    Cancel with 7+ days' notice and your deposit moves to new dates or comes straight back. Under 7 days we keep the deposit — nothing more, ever. Washed out mid-stay? We'll credit unused nights.

Dates in mind already?

Ring reception and a human will check the board while you're on the phone. School holidays go early; May and September are the locals' secret.
