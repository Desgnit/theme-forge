Finding us — sat-nav warning

Wayside Meadow, Hawsker Lane, near Whitby YO22 4LT. Some sat-navs send caravans down Chapel Lane — don't follow it, it's single-track with no turning space. Stay on the A171 to the village, then follow our brown tent-and-caravan signs. The last half mile is narrow but fine if you keep to the signed route; passing places every hundred yards or so.

Site rules, in short

Quiet from 10pm (gates locked) to 7:30am. No groups, no music beyond your own pitch, campfires in our fire bowls only, dogs on leads except the dog field, 5mph everywhere. The full rules fit on one sheet — you'll get it at check-in.

Wayside Meadow

Family-run caravan and camping site on the cliffs near Whitby since 1989. Sixty pitches, sea views, and a shop that never runs out of camping gas.
