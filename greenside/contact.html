Find the green

Visiting Orchard Lane

  • By car: free parking at the gate on Orchard Lane, with two accessible bays beside the clubhouse door.
  • By bus: the number 14 stops right outside; from the town centre it's a level ten-minute walk.
  • Green fees: visitors £5 a session, bowls loaned free — just sign the book in the clubhouse. Watching costs nothing at all.

Open days & clubhouse hire

Open days run on the first Saturday of May and June, 10am–4pm — have-a-go rinks on both greens, coaches on hand and cream teas from the ladies' team. The clubhouse (seats 80) is available to hire for birthdays, wakes and quiz nights; members get mates' rates.
