More than the league team

Four teams, one badge

Saturday league team

Six rinks of four in the Three Counties League every Saturday afternoon. Runners-up last season — the closest finish in twenty years.

Midweek triples & ladies' team

Wednesday-evening triples for anyone who fancies a gentler game, and a ladies' team who have just won the county shield — again.

Newcomers' roll-up

Tuesday mornings and Thursday evenings, all summer. No experience, no equipment, no pressure — just come along and have a bowl.

New bowlers

Never held a bowl? Perfect.

Half our members had never played before they walked through the gate. Free coaching taster sessions run all summer on the flat green — we lend you the bowls, teach you the etiquette as you go, and nobody keeps score until you want to.

  • Free taster sessions with Bowls England qualified coaches
  • Club bowls loaned in every size — flat-soled shoes are the only requirement
  • Tuesday mornings or Thursday evenings — tea and biscuits included
